Motivation To Stop Scale (MTSS)

The Motivation To Stop Scale (MTSS) is a single-item measure that assesses your current motivation and readiness to quit smoking. Please select the option that best describes your current thoughts about smoking cessation.

Your MTSS Results

Score: 0/7

What your score means:

  • 1-2: Very Low Motivation (Precontemplation stage) - You're not currently considering quitting smoking.
  • 3-4: Moderate Motivation (Contemplation stage) - You're thinking about quitting but haven't made concrete plans.
  • 5-7: High Motivation (Preparation stage) - You're motivated to quit and are making or planning to make specific steps toward quitting.
